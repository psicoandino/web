# LEXICON.md

## Core Vocabulary

This lexicon defines the permanent language of Project Emergence.

Every future interface, API, and interaction should use these concepts consistently.

---

## Expression

The editable manifestation of a thought.

Expression exists only while a thought is being formed.

It is temporary.

It has no permanent identity.

Expression becomes an Emergence only through Seal.

---

## Emergence 🌱

An Emergence is a sealed Expression.

It is the first permanent manifestation of a thought.

An Emergence possesses identity.

It becomes the root of every future Descendant.

It can never be edited.

---

## Descendant

A new thought that acknowledges the existence of a previous one.

A Descendant preserves ancestry while allowing evolution.

Every Descendant may itself become the parent of future descendants.

---

## Seal

Seal transforms an Expression into an Emergence.

Seal is recognition.

Not saving.

Not publishing.

Not committing.

Once sealed,

the Emergence becomes immutable.

---

## Reflection 🌿

A period of deliberate contemplation.

Reflection represents the stage in which thoughts begin interacting with one another.

Meaning deepens without requiring publication.

---

## Published 📖

A publicly shared projection of one node in a genealogy.

Publication never ends evolution.

It simply makes one moment visible.

---

## Genealogy

The complete ancestry of an Emergence and all of its descendants.

Genealogy is the primary structure of the system.

---

## Lineage

A continuous path through a genealogy.

Multiple lineages may coexist.

None are inherently superior.

---

## Branch

A visual description of divergence.

Branch is an implementation detail.

Descendant is the conceptual primitive.

---

## Document

A temporary arrangement of selected thoughts.

Documents are outputs.

They are not the source of truth.
