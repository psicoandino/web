# FOUNDATION.md

## Project Emergence

### Version 0.1

> *A thought, once sealed, can never be lost. Every future thought acknowledges its ancestry instead of replacing it.*

---

# Purpose

Project Emergence exists to preserve the evolution of thought.

Traditional writing systems optimize documents.

Project Emergence optimizes the genealogy of ideas.

A document is no longer the primary object.

An evolving thought is.

---

# First Principles

## P1 — Thought precedes document.

Documents are temporary arrangements of thoughts.

Thoughts are not temporary arrangements of documents.

---

## P2 — The first expression has intrinsic value.

The first manifestation of an idea is not an inferior draft.

It is evidence of how the idea first appeared.

Its value is historical, cognitive, and creative.

---

## P3 — Nothing meaningful is overwritten.

Once a thought is sealed, it becomes immutable.

Future refinement never replaces the past.

It acknowledges it.


### P3.1 — Expression precedes Emergence.

An Expression is the editable manifestation of a thought.

An Expression becomes an Emergence only when it is Sealed.

Only Emergences become immutable historical artifacts.

Expressions are transient.

Emergences are permanent.

---

## P4 — Evolution replaces revision.

Writing is treated as evolution rather than correction.

Every meaningful modification creates lineage.

---

## P5 — History is a first-class citizen.

History is not metadata.

History is part of the work itself.

---

## P6 — Lineage is more important than chronology.

The system does not merely remember when something happened.

It remembers where every idea came from.

---

## P7 — Exploration is preferred over convergence.

Multiple descendants may coexist indefinitely.

The objective is understanding.

Not producing a single canonical version.

---

## P8 — Publication is projection.

Publishing does not terminate evolution.

A published work is merely one visible projection of an ongoing genealogy.

---

## P9 — The writer owns the pace of evolution.

The system never forces convergence.

The writer decides whether to continue a lineage, branch into a descendant, or publish.

---

## P10 — Technology is replaceable.

These principles must remain valid regardless of programming language, storage engine, interface, or platform.

The philosophy precedes the implementation.

---

# Non-Goals

Project Emergence is not:

* another note-taking application
* another markdown editor
* another document processor
* Git adapted for writers
* social media
* collaborative editing software

These may become implementations.

They are not the identity.
